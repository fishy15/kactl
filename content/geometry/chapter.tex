        from two points $(x_1, y_1)$ and $(x_2, y_2)$:

    To project points onto a plane
        and map plane to $xy$-plane,
        project point onto the plane,
        use normalized $(B - A)$ and $(C - A)$ as unit vectors,
        and find projected point as linear combination of basis vectors on $xy$-plane.
    $(B - A)$ maps to $(1, 0)$,
        $(C - A)$ maps to $(\cos \theta, \sin \theta)$,
        where $\theta$ is the angle between $B - A$ and $C - A$.
